# NFT SaaS — WordPress Plugin

Sell your digital art as NFTs directly from WordPress. Buyers pay on-chain — you receive **98% of every sale** automatically.

📖 **Full setup guide:** [docs/WORDPRESS_PLUGIN.md](https://github.com/spntnhub/nft-saas/blob/main/docs/WORDPRESS_PLUGIN.md)

---

## Quick Start

1. Download `nft-saas.zip` → upload via **Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin**
2. Go to **NFT SaaS → Settings**
3. Click **"Don't have one? Click here to generate instantly"** → enter email → **Get API Key**
4. Click **Save Settings**
5. Add the **NFT Buy Button** block to any post

---

## How It Works

- **Lazy minting** — NFTs are minted on-chain only when someone buys. No upfront cost for you.
- **Buyer pays gas** — all transaction fees are paid by the buyer at purchase time.
- **Instant payout** — 98% of the sale amount goes directly to your wallet via the smart contract.
- **2% royalty** — automatically collected by the platform on every sale and resale (ERC-2981).
